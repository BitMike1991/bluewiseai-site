A simple ROI calculator for AI automation

Use this framework to decide whether a project is worth the investment:

üîß ROI Mini‚ÄëFramework

  1. Estimate time saved per week. Identify the hours spent on the current manual process.
  2. Assign an hourly value. Use your billable rate or the cost of the employee performing the task.
  3. Estimate the cost of automation. Include setup time, external help and monthly subscription fees.
  4. Compute payback period. Divide total cost by the monthly time savings in dollars or euros.

For example, saving 5¬†hours per week at $80 per hour generates $400/week or about $1,600/month. If setup costs $2,000 and tools cost $100/month, you recoup your investment in under two months.
